# The Amulet of Ages: A Text-Based RPG

## üìñ Concept

Welcome to "The Amulet of Ages," a text-based roleplaying adventure! You are an adventurer tasked with investigating a spreading darkness that threatens the kingdom. Your journey will take you from haunted forests to the imposing castle of a cursed king, where your choices will determine the fate of the land.

## ‚ú® Features

* **Character Creation**: Choose from three unique classes: the stalwart **Warrior**, the cunning **Rogue**, or the powerful **Mage**.
* **Dynamic Stats**: Your stats (Strength, Agility, Magic) directly influence combat and your ability to overcome obstacles.
* **Turn-Based Combat**: Engage in classic turn-based combat. A simple dice-roll mechanic (1-20) determines the success of your actions.
* **Inventory System**: Collect, manage, and use items, from healing potions to legendary weapons and quest artifacts.
* **Branching Narrative**: Your decisions matter. The game features multiple unique locations and decision points that lead to one of three distinct endings.
* **Rich World**: Explore a multi-stage story that takes you through the Whispering Woods, the Shadowy Lair, and the Cursed Keep.

## ‚å®Ô∏è Basic Gameplay Commands

* `go [direction]` (e.g., `go north`, `go forest`)
* `look` (or `l`): Examine your current surroundings.
* `take [item]` (e.g., `take potion`): Pick up an item.
* `use [item]` (e.g., `use potion`): Use an item from your inventory.
* `inventory` (or `i`): Check your inventory.
* `attack [enemy]` (e.g., `attack goblin`): Initiate combat or attack on your turn.
* `defend`: Take a defensive stance during combat to reduce incoming damage.
* `magic`: Use a magic spell during combat (if you are a Mage).
* `talk [character]` (e.g., `talk ghost`): Speak to an NPC.
* `help`: Display a list of available commands.
* `quit`: Exit the game.
